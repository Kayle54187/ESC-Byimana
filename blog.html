About ESCBYIMANA

Ecole Des Sciences Byimana, a science school founded in 1952 by Marist Brothers in Rwanda. The school aims to provide inclusive and holistic education grounded in Christian values. It highlights the school's culture, spirit, leadership history, testimonials, recent posts, and the principal's message.
